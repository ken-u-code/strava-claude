**Neural flossing protocol (non-negotiable):**
2 sets × 8–10 reps. Gentle — never into pain. After every hard session and before bed. Prevents tibial nerve recurrence.

**Prevention principles:**
- Calf raises weekly — foot intrinsic strength
- Shoe rotation — prevents neuromuscular adaptation to single geometry
- No aggressive hamstring stretching during nerve flare
- No direct calf foam rolling during flare
- Flat easy running only during nerve sensitivity
